Platform Features

Built Right.
From the Start.

📱

Progressive Web App — No Download Required

Students access PocketQuiz directly from any browser. Install-to-homescreen support on iOS and Android. No App Store approval cycle, no platform gatekeeping.

🔌

Offline-First Architecture

Question banks cache locally on first load. Students in low-connectivity environments — study halls, transit, rural areas — get the full experience regardless of signal.

🎯

Adaptive Difficulty Tiers

Each subject's 200 questions are distributed across introductory, intermediate, and advanced tiers. The app surfaces questions matched to the student's demonstrated performance level.

🎨

Lily & Quinn Character System

Two SVG-illustrated characters in a Hanna-Barbera-inspired style guide students through sessions. Animated reactions to correct and incorrect answers reinforce positive feedback loops.

📊

Session Tracking & Progress

Per-subject scores, streak counters, and session history stored locally. No account required for core functionality — privacy-first design with optional cloud sync planned for v2.

🏫

Institutional Licensing Path

Designed for campus-wide deployment. Administrators can white-label the platform, add institution-specific question banks, and track cohort-level performance via a dashboard add-on.

Meet the Characters

Lily & Quinn

Two characters designed in SVG for infinite scalability and smooth animation — styled after the golden era of Saturday morning cartoons. Every question answered gets a reaction. Every streak gets celebrated.

The Encourager

Lily

Warm, enthusiastic, and relentlessly positive. Lily celebrates every correct answer and gently redirects wrong ones. She specializes in life sciences and humanities, appearing as the primary guide for Biology, Earth Science, and History sessions. Her design features rounded forms, expressive eyes, and a signature rose-pink palette.

The Analyst

Quinn

Sharp, curious, and methodical. Quinn breaks down complex problems step by step and rewards careful thinking. He leads STEM subjects — Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science. His angular design and cool blue palette signal precision without intimidation.
