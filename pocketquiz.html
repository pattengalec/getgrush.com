Characters

Lily & Quinn.
Your Study Crew.

🌸 Lily
Outgoing · Impetuous · Acts first, mid-air plan

Caramel hair ponytail with a coral scrunchie. Coral L-shirt, mismatched laces (one coral, one blue). Lily launches into answers with full body energy — star eyes on correct, rubber-collapse on wrong. When she nails it, the whole stage knows.

🎨 Quinn
Artistic · Introverted · Already drew the answer

Wheat locs with a pencil tucked in. Sage Q-shirt, sketchbook bag with marker doodles on the toe cap, embroidered knee patch with a little flower. Quinn's default is a knowing half-smile. Wrong answer gets The Look — single squinted eye that says everything.

Progressive Web App

Install-optional. Works in any browser. Offline-first architecture means students can study without a connection — on the bus, in the waiting room, anywhere.

🎮

Animated Character Feedback

Lily and Quinn react to every answer with full Hanna-Barbera physics — idle bounce, star eyes on correct, rubber-body collapse on wrong, Stimpy grin on streaks.

🔊

Web Audio Engine

Zero audio files. Procedurally generated sound effects via Web Audio API — ascending xylophone on correct, descending trombone wobble on wrong, escalating boings on streaks.

📚

Subject-Specific Question Banks

1,400+ questions across 7 STEM subjects, written at high school and introductory college level. Chemistry complete. Biology, Math, Physics, Earth Science, CS, and History in production.

🏫

Institutional Licensing

Built for campus deployment. Single license covers a department or school. Grush integration available for tracking student engagement by zone and session.
