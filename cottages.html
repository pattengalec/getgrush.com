Process

Configure.
Build. Deliver.

No construction permits for mobile units. No contractor coordination. You select a model, configure your layout, and take delivery of a business-ready structure.

1
Select Model

Choose MYCOttage, GROWcottage, or SHOPcottage. Select container size and layout configuration via the Cottage Configurator.

2
Configure

Specify electrical load, climate systems, interior fit-out, and exterior finish. Every build is documented before fabrication begins.

3
Fabricate

Container is modified, fitted, wired, and finished in the shop. Systems are tested before the unit leaves the build site.

4
Deliver

Unit is trucked and placed on site. Connect utilities, load your product, and operate. Grush zone tracking is pre-installed on every build.

Proof of Concept

CBU Campus:
MYCOttage Pilot

California Baptist University — Lancer Farms & Gardens

A MYCOttage unit is slated for deployment at CBU's Lancer Farms & Gardens in Riverside, CA as the institutional proof-of-concept before graduation. The unit will operate as an active mushroom cultivation system on campus, demonstrating the full MYCOttage workflow — inoculation, colonization, fruiting, and harvest — under real institutional conditions. Grush zone tracking is pre-integrated. The CBU deployment establishes the first documented institutional reference for the Cottage product line.
