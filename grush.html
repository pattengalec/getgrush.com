How It Works

Scan. Log. Report.

Three steps. No dedicated app. No hardware installation. Any smartphone with a camera is the entire client.

01
Print & Place

Generate unique QR codes for each zone in the admin panel. Print and post — no power, no Wi-Fi required at the zone itself.

02
Scan & Submit

Staff, students, or volunteers scan the QR with any phone camera. A Google Form pre-populated with the zone ID opens instantly. Activity logged in seconds.

03
Review & Report

All submissions flow into a live Google Sheet dashboard. Filter by zone, date, or user. Export or share with supervisors and stakeholders in one click.

Live Deployment

Institutional Pilot:
CBU Lancer Farms

California Baptist University — Lancer Farms & Gardens

Grush is fully operational at CBU's campus farm in Riverside, CA. Student volunteers and farm staff use QR zone codes daily to log activity across planting beds, irrigation zones, composting areas, and storage. The system runs on Google Apps Script with a live Sheets dashboard reviewed by farm leadership — zero ongoing licensing cost, zero app installs.
